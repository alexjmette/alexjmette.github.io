Caspian Sea-Level Model
Model of sea-level change built using an environmental modeling software. The model incorporated inflows from rivers and precipitation and outflows from evaporation to predict changes in sea-level. Evaporation rates varied based on surface area, temperature, and salinity. Ultimately, the goal of the model is to provide a way for decision-makers to understand the impact of restricting inflows to the Sea through dams on the Volga river, which represents 80% of the Caspian’s inflow.

Social Impact Bond: Girls Education in India
In 2013, while working as an LGT Venture Philanthropy Fellow with Educate Girls, an organization dedicated to eliminating the education gender gap in India, I contributed to the world’s first development impact bond in education. A social impact bond or development impact bond is a type of payment by results financing mechanism that brings together actors from the public and private sectors to fund positive social outcomes.
